About the Theatre

The Carousel Theatre is an 1800s renovated cinema. This family run business has been open for 25 years and has solidified itself as one of Blackwood's go-to spots, seating 504 people in 4 screens.

Where to find us

You can find us on Blackwood High Street! We are opposite Papa John's Pizza.
